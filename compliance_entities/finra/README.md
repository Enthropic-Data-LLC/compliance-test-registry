# FINRA — Financial Industry Regulatory Authority

**Authority:** FINRA (self-regulatory organization); rules approved by SEC; enforcement via FINRA Department of Enforcement
**Scope:** FINRA-member broker-dealers and their associated persons; supervisory systems (FINRA Rule 3110), books and records (Rule 4511), customer protection (Rule 4370), cybersecurity (Regulatory Notice 15-09)
