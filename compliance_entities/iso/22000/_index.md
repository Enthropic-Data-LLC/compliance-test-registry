## Hazard Control Plan (HACCP) — required elements (DETERMINISTIC)

For each CCP and OPRP in the Hazard Control Plan:
1. Food safety hazard(s) controlled
2. Control measure(s)
3. Critical limits (CCPs) or action criteria (OPRPs)
4. Monitoring procedures and frequency
5. Corrective actions when limits are exceeded
6. Responsibilities and authorities
7. Records of monitoring

All 7 elements must be documented. Record existence is DETERMINISTIC; content appropriateness is PARAMETERIZED.
