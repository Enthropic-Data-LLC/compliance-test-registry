# COPPA — Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

**Authority:** Federal Trade Commission (FTC); 16 CFR Part 312
**Scope:** Operators of commercial websites and online services directed to children under 13, or operators with actual knowledge they are collecting personal information from children under 13
