# NADCAP — National Aerospace and Defense Contractors Accreditation Program

**Authority:** Performance Review Institute (PRI) on behalf of the NADCAP Management Committee (major aerospace OEMs)
**Scope:** Special processes in aerospace and defense manufacturing: heat treatment, welding, chemical processing, NDT, composites, coatings, electronics, materials testing, and more; accreditation required by Boeing, Airbus, Lockheed Martin, Raytheon, and most other primes
