# NRC 10 CFR Part 50 — Domestic Licensing of Production and Utilization Facilities

**Authority:** U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC)
**Scope:** Commercial nuclear power reactors licensed under 10 CFR Part 50 (construction permits + operating licenses) and Part 52 (combined licenses); primary safety, QA, and operational requirements
**Enforcement:** ~90 operating reactors; NRC resident inspectors on-site at each plant; Reactor Oversight Process (ROP) with Significance Determination Process (SDP)
