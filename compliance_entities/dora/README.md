## Key DETERMINISTIC thresholds

| Obligation | Threshold | Article |
|---|---|---|
| Initial incident notification | 4 hours from classification as major; ≤24 hours from awareness (earlier governs) | Art. 19(4)(a) |
| Intermediate incident report | 72 hours from initial notification | Art. 19(4)(b) |
| Final incident report | 1 month from intermediate report | Art. 19(4)(c) |
| Basic resilience testing | At least annually (all 4 required components) | Art. 25 |
| TLPT (significant entities) | Every 3 years from prior TLPT completion date | Art. 26 |
| Backup restore test | At least annually; must demonstrate achievement of documented RTO | Art. 12(5) |
| Backup geographic separation | Required — separate from primary site | Art. 12(1) |
| ICT contract mandatory elements | 12 elements — all must be present (critical/important functions) | Art. 30(2) |
| BCP/DRP annual review | At least annually | Art. 11 |
| ICT risk framework board approval | Management body approval required | Art. 5(2) |
| TPSP register currency | Annual review minimum | Art. 28(3) |
