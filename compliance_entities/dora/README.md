# DORA — EU Digital Operational Resilience Act (Regulation 2022/2554)

**Authority:** Joint Committee of European Supervisory Authorities (EBA, EIOPA, ESMA); national competent authorities per member state; applies from January 17, 2025
**Scope:** Financial entities in the EU (banks, insurers, investment firms, crypto-asset service providers, payment institutions) and critical ICT third-party providers; 5 pillars: ICT risk management, incident classification/reporting, resilience testing, third-party risk, information sharing
