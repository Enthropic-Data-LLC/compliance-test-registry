# CCPA / CPRA — California Consumer Privacy Act / Privacy Rights Act

**Authority:** California Privacy Protection Agency (CPPA); California Attorney General (AG)
**Scope:** For-profit businesses meeting threshold criteria (revenue ≥$25M, or data on ≥100K consumers/households, or ≥50% of revenue from selling personal information) that do business in California
