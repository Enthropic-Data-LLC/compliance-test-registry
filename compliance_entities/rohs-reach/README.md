# RoHS / REACH — EU Chemical Restriction Regulations

**Authority:** European Commission; EU member state market surveillance authorities (REACH: ECHA)
**Scope:** RoHS (Directive 2011/65/EU + 2015/863/EU): restriction of hazardous substances (lead, mercury, cadmium, Cr6+, PBBs, PBDEs, DEHP, BBP, DBP, DIBP) in electrical and electronic equipment placed on the EU market | REACH (Regulation 1907/2006): registration, evaluation, authorization, and restriction of chemicals manufactured/imported into the EU ≥1 tonne/year
