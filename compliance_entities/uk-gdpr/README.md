# UK GDPR + Data Protection Act 2018

**Authority:** Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
**Scope:** Processing of personal data of UK data subjects; applies to controllers and processors established in the UK and to non-UK entities offering goods/services to UK data subjects or monitoring their behavior; note: UK GDPR diverged from EU GDPR post-Brexit with UK-specific adequacy decisions and transfer mechanisms
