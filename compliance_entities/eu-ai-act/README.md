# EU AI Act — Regulation (EU) 2024/1689

**Authority:** European AI Office (EAIO); national market surveillance authorities; applies in phases from 2024–2027
**Scope:** AI systems placed on or put into service in the EU; providers, deployers, importers, and distributors of AI systems; risk-based framework (Unacceptable / High / Limited / Minimal risk)
