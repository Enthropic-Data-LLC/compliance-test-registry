# CMMC 2.0 — Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification

**Authority:** U.S. Department of Defense (DoD); 32 CFR Part 170
**Scope:** DoD contractors and subcontractors handling Federal Contract Information (FCI) or Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI)

## Levels

| Level | Name | Practices | Assessment |
|---|---|---|---|
| Level 1 | Foundational | 17 (FAR 52.204-21) | Annual self-assessment |
| Level 2 | Advanced | 110 (NIST SP 800-171 Rev 2) | Triennial third-party (C3PAO) or annual self |
| Level 3 | Expert | 110 + 24 (NIST SP 800-172) | Triennial government-led |
